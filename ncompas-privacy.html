Privacy Policy

NCompas Business - TikTok Analytics
Effective Date: November 7, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: NCompas Business is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your data when you use our TikTok Analytics service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use NCompas TikTok Analytics, we collect and process the following types of information:

a) TikTok Account Information

  • Profile information (username, display name, bio, profile picture)
  • Account statistics (follower count, following count, likes count, video count)
  • Verification status and account type
  • Profile and account URLs

b) Video Content Data

  • Public video information (titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
  • Video performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)
  • Engagement statistics and completion rates
  • Publication dates and timestamps
  • Video metadata (duration, format, dimensions)

c) Usage and Analytics Data

  • Service usage patterns and preferences
  • Feature interactions and session information
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Access times and duration

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Analytics Services: Generate comprehensive analytics reports, performance insights, and data visualizations
  • Calculate Metrics: Process data to compute engagement rates, growth trends, audience demographics, and performance indicators
  • Improve Our Service: Enhance functionality, develop new features, and optimize user experience
  • Personalization: Customize analytics displays and recommendations based on your content type
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send service updates, feature announcements, and support responses
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations ensure only authorized personnel can access your data
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our systems are hosted on secure, regularly audited cloud platforms
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Regular backups ensure data integrity and recovery capabilities

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in service delivery (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics tools) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
  • Protection: To protect rights, property, or safety of NCompas, users, or the public

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • TikTok Platform: We access TikTok data through official APIs subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • Azure AI Services: For sentiment analysis and natural language processing (optional feature)
  • Analytics Tools: To improve our service and understand usage patterns

These third parties have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Revoke Authorization: Disconnect our service from your TikTok account at any time
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Object: Object to specific data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ncompasbusiness.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our analytics services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When you disconnect our service or request deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately for removal.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user sessions and preferences
  • Analyze service usage and performance
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notification to registered users

The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting with a new "Effective Date."

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used, and the right to request deletion.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)
